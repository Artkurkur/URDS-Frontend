Our Services

The URDS System provides essential research management services to streamline proposal handling, evaluation, and communication across the university.

  • Proposal Submission: Faculty Researchers can submit research proposals for review and evaluation.
  • Proposal Evaluation: Senior Faculty Researchers and Evaluators assess, rate, and provide comments on proposals.
  • Proposal Revision & Resubmission: Researchers may revise and resubmit their proposals based on TWG feedback.
  • Announcement Dissemination: College Deans and Research Coordinators publish and distribute announcements to faculty.
  • Automated Notifications: Users are automatically notified of updates, reviews, and decisions.

About the URDS System

The URDS System (University Research and Development Services) is designed to enhance the research management process within the university.

It connects various academic stakeholders — from the URDS Director, College Deans, and Research Coordinators to Faculty Researchers and Evaluators — ensuring that proposals move efficiently from submission to approval.

System Participants:

  • URDS Director: Announces and receives research proposals.
  • College Dean: Evaluates proposals, provides feedback, and disseminates announcements.
  • Research Coordinator: Forwards proposals and announcements to the faculty.
  • Faculty Researchers: Submit, revise, and defend research proposals.
  • Evaluators: Approve, rate, or terminate proposals based on criteria.
